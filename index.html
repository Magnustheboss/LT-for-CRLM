Key Takeaways for Clinical Practice

1

LT is now a validated standard option

The TransMet RCT demonstrates that LT plus chemotherapy significantly improves 5-year OS (56.6% vs. 12.6%) compared to chemotherapy alone in permanently unresectable CRLM.

2

Rigorous patient selection is essential

Use of the Oslo Score, FCRS, PET-MTV, and molecular profiling (especially RAS/TP53 status) ensures selection of patients with favorable tumor biology.

3

Recurrence is common but manageable

While DFS is low, median OS after relapse is 37.1 months, suggesting indolent disease biology in selected patients.

4

LDLT expands access

Living donor LT circumvents deceased donor scarcity and offers comparable or superior outcomes.
