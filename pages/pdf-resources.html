How to Use These Resources

For Clinical Practice

  1. Download the Quick Reference Guide and keep it at your desk
  2. Use the calculators when evaluating new patients
  3. Print the evaluation checklist for your team
  4. Share the patient information sheet with candidates

For Education

  1. Use the Comprehensive Guide for resident/fellow education
  2. Print case studies for journal clubs
  3. Share survival data for presentations
  4. Use infographics in teaching rounds

For Patient Counseling

  1. Provide the Patient Information Sheet at first consultation
  2. Use survival curves when discussing outcomes
  3. Review the evaluation checklist to explain the process
  4. Discuss realistic expectations using evidence-based data
